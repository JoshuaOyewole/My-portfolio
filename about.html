About Me

Hey, I'm Joshua Oyewole.

I started SWD in 2015. My focus was to generate a side-income while I was working a full-time job and this website started off as a resource for others interested in learning web development.

I soon found out that there were thousands in the same boat as me, but they found it hard to get clients and to earn a consistent income. After 12 months of learning and working with clients, I earned more than my full-time income and I eventually left to freelance full-time

This brings us to today, where I'm fortunate enough to share what I know after freelancing for over 6 years. I'm focused on making SWD the go-to resource for web developers wanting to become freelancers. Web development and freelancing changed my life and I hope the same for you.

If you're new here, start by reading the Blog or TheFreelancerPodcast.com. Either way, it's an honour to partner with you on your web development and freelancing journey.
